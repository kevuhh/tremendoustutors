Mission Statement

Tremendous Tutors is dedicated to providing free, need-based academic assistance to students in middle and high school in all subjects. Tremendous Tutors prioritizes low-income students but is available all students.

Are tutoring sessions virtual or in person?

Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, all tutoring sessions are currently being held virtually (via Zoom).

Are the tutoring sessions a one-time thing or recurring?

They can be either! When you sign up, you can select whether you want to sign up for one session, recurring sessions, or if you are unsure!

Are the tutors paid or volunteers?

All tutors are high school volunteers, but theyall meet the rigorous standards Tremendous Tutors sets in terms of academics and teaching experience.

I've signed up for tutoring! Now what?

Your tutor will reach out to you shortly via email to coordinate a time that works for both of you for your session(s).

What subjects can you tutor in?

On the sign-up form there is an extensive list of subjects available to choose from. If you need assistance in a subject not currently listed, you can manually input the subject into the "other" section, and we will try our best to accomodate you.
